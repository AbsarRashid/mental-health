Mission

Our mission is to promote optimal mental health through counselling, advice, experiences and support.

Vision

Everyone gets the personalised services and support they need, fight mental illness and lead a healthy life

Our Goals

  • You matter believes that people can improve their own lives when provided with the necessary skills, knowledge, and access to resources.
  • It emphasizes action and measurable results in the lives of the people it seeks to help.
  • You Too values the courage to break new ground, fill vacuums, and address the most difficult problems in the most difficult situations.
  • You Too recognizes that mental health is the most important thing for a healthy life and it can be improved with a little willingness to explore the in-depth problems.
  • You Matter aims to help people who have suicidal thoughts and encourage them to see a beautiful day.
